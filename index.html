About

MDL Aerial is operated by Maurizio De Luca, an FAA Part 107 certified remote pilot based in the Los Angeles area.

Maurizio also holds an FAA Commercial Pilot Certificate for manned aircraft, bringing a strong aviation background and a safety-focused approach to drone operations.

MDL Aerial specializes in aerial mapping, photogrammetry, and high-resolution drone documentation for buildings, properties, inspections, and visual project records.
